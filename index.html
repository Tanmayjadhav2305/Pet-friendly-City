About Pet-Friendly City

We're on a mission to transform cities into havens where pets and their humans can thrive together. Our initiative focuses on:

  • Creating more pet-friendly parks
  • Supporting pet-friendly businesses
  • Advocating for pet-inclusive housing
  • Organizing pet-friendly events
